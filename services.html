Traditional & Ayurveda Treatments at Erundeniya Ayurveda Hospital

At Erundenia Traditional Ayurveda Hospital, we offer a wide range of specialized traditional and Ayurveda treatments deeply rooted in our family’s time-honored medical heritage.

Sciatica Treatments

A specialized combination of indigenous and Ayurvedic therapies focusing on relieving inflammation, reducing nerve compression, and restoring flexibility along the sciatic nerve.

Benefits:

Reduces shooting pain in the lower back, hips, and legs; improves posture; enhances mobility.

Suitable For:

Patients suffering from sciatica, chronic lower back pain, and nerve-related leg pain.

Koshtuka Shiro Therapy

A targeted head and spinal base treatment using warm medicated herbal oils and gentle massage techniques.

Benefits:

Relieves neck and shoulder stiffness, reduces headaches, and enhances blood circulation to the brain.

Suitable For:

Tension headaches, migraines, cervical spondylosis, and stress-related discomfort.

Shirodhara (Herbal Oil Pouring Therapy on Forehead)

A continuous flow of warm herbal oil is poured onto the forehead in a rhythmic pattern to calm the mind and nervous system.

Benefits:

Deep relaxation, reduced anxiety, improved sleep quality, and enhanced mental clarity.

Suitable For:

Insomnia, anxiety disorders, mental fatigue, depression, and migraine sufferers.

Netra Vasti (Herbal Oil Retention for Eyes)

Warm herbal ghee or medicated oil is held over the eyes in a natural dough barrier to nourish and rejuvenate eye tissues.

Benefits:

Improves vision, relieves eye strain, and prevents degenerative eye conditions.

Suitable For:

Dry eyes, redness, burning sensation, eye fatigue from computer work, and early-stage cataracts.

Karna Poorana (Herbal Ear Oil Therapy)

Gently filling the ear canal with warm, medicated oil to lubricate and cleanse the inner ear.

Benefits:

Improves hearing, prevents ear infections, reduces ringing in the ears (tinnitus).

Suitable For:

Chronic earaches, tinnitus, partial hearing loss, and dizziness related to ear imbalance.

Nasya (Nasal Cleansing Therapy)

Administration of herbal oils or juices through the nostrils to cleanse the nasal passages and sinus region.

Benefits:

Clears sinus congestion, sharpens memory, improves respiratory health.

Suitable For:

Sinusitis, nasal allergies, chronic headaches, and memory weakness.

Hasta Anguli & Pada Anguli Therapies

Traditional finger and toe massage/manipulation to stimulate energy points and improve circulation.

Benefits:

Improves joint flexibility, relieves stiffness, enhances nerve health.

Suitable For:

Arthritis, nerve numbness, joint pain, and circulation issues.

Hisahori Therapy (Traditional Head Therapy)

Indigenous head care combining herbal paste application, massage, and steam for deep relaxation and scalp nourishment.

Benefits:

Promotes hair growth, reduces headaches, and relieves mental stress.

Suitable For:

Hair loss, chronic stress, scalp conditions, and tension headaches.

Abhyanga (Full Body Massage with Medicated Oils)

A warm oil massage covering the entire body to rejuvenate tissues and improve overall health.

Benefits:

Improves circulation, nourishes skin, boosts immunity, and relieves fatigue.

Suitable For:

General wellness, muscle stiffness, and chronic fatigue.

Shirolepa (Herbal Paste Application on Head)

Application of cooling herbal paste over the head to reduce inflammation and tension.

Benefits:

Relieves migraines, promotes restful sleep, and calms the nervous system.

Suitable For:

Migraine, stress, and insomnia.

Steam Bath (Bashpa Sweda)

Exposure to herbal steam to open pores and eliminate toxins through sweating. Benefits: Improves skin health, relieves joint stiffness, and enhances detoxification.

Benefits:

Improves skin health, relieves joint stiffness, and enhances detoxification.

Suitable For:

Rheumatism, skin disorders, and obesity.

Thalam (Herbal Paste Application on Crown)

Applying herbal paste to the crown area of the head for cooling and mental balance.

Benefits:

Relieves stress, reduces headaches, and enhances mental clarity.

Suitable For:

Chronic headaches, stress, and insomnia.

Netra Dhara (Herbal Decoction Pouring Over Eyes)

Pouring herbal decoction continuously over the eyes to cleanse and rejuvenate.

Benefits:

Improves eye health, reduces inflammation, and relieves strain.

Suitable For:

Eye infections, irritation, and fatigue.

Hrid Vasti (Herbal Oil Retention Over Chest)

Warm herbal oil is retained in a dough ring over the chest to nourish and support heart function.

Benefits:

Improves circulation, reduces chest tightness, and relieves emotional stress.

Suitable For:

Heart-related ailments, asthma, and emotional imbalance.

Sinus Purification Procedures

Traditional cleansing methods to clear sinus blockages and reduce inflammation.

Benefits:

Improves breathing, reduces headaches, and prevents sinus infections.

Suitable For:

Sinusitis, nasal congestion, and chronic headaches.

Hemorrhoid Management (Arshas Pratikara)

Herbal and dietary methods to shrink hemorrhoids and prevent recurrence.

Benefits:

Relieves pain, reduces swelling, and improves bowel health.

Suitable For:

Piles, anal fissures, and chronic constipation.

Uterine-Related Treatments

Herbal and therapeutic procedures for women's reproductive health.

Benefits:

Regulates menstrual cycles, strengthens the uterus, and supports postnatal recovery.

Suitable For:

Menstrual disorders, infertility, and postpartum care.

Skin Condition Treatments

Herbal therapies targeting both internal and external causes of skin diseases.

Benefits:

Clears acne, reduces inflammation, and promotes natural glow.

Suitable For:

Eczema, psoriasis, acne, and pigmentation.

Nila Therapy (Exclusive to Erundeniya Ayurveda)

A signature therapy passed down through generations, using a secret blend of herbs for chronic and rare conditions.

Benefits:

Highly effective in certain cases where conventional medicine has shown limited results.

Suitable For:

Chronic, hard-to-treat illnesses, and specific rare conditions.

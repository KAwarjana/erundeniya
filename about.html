Vision

Recognizing health as the ultimate wealth, we strive to bring joy and healing to those who are physically and mentally afflicted, guiding them towards a vibrant, healthy future through the provision of time-honored traditional practices and highly effective natural medicines. We aim to empower individuals to experience the fullness of life and its sustaining universal energy.

Mission

To enhance the health, longevity, and well-being of Sri Lankans by integrating the ancient wisdom of Hela Wedakama, the holistic principles of Ayurveda, and the esteemed spiritual arts of Sri Lankan heritage. Our mission is to cultivate a nation renowned for vitality, wisdom, and natural well-being — fostering life, strength, comfort, and inner peace. Moreover, we are dedicated to preserving, honoring, and sharing these invaluable traditions with the world. Ultimately, we aspire to establish a distinguished medical institution where medicine, compassionate care, spirituality, and humanity are nurtured together in perfect harmony.

Biography

Dr. H.D.P. Darshani is a sixth-generation Ayurveda (Traditional Indigenous Ayurveda) practitioner, continuing a respected family legacy of healing that spans over five generations. With over 30 years of professional experience, she serves as the Chief Consultant at the Erundeniya Deshiya Paramparika Ayurveda Hospital, where she practices as a fully qualified Sammuthiya Weda Mahattmaya (licensed indigenous physician).

Her medical heritage, deeply rooted in Sri Lanka’s traditional healing wisdom, equips her with unique expertise in treating a wide range of complex and chronic conditions. These include:

  •    Rheumatic and joint diseases
  •    Liver disorders
  •    Neurological conditions
  •    Kidney diseases
  •    Cardiac ailments
  •    Urinary tract infections
  •    Gynecological and postnatal conditions
  •    Fistula (Bhagandara)
  •    Eye diseases
  •    Intestinal issues
  •    Fractures and dislocations
  •    Tumors including benign and certain types of malignant growths

In response to modern health challenges, Dr. Darshani has also developed her own traditional formulations, including treatments for emerging diseases like COVID-19, using time-honored indigenous ingredients combined with modern insight.

In a testament to her vision of preserving and advancing Deshiya Paramparika Weda Kama (traditional medicine), she has guided her son and daughter to follow in her footsteps. Both are now serving as Ayurvedic doctors, ensuring the continuation of this healing tradition into the future. Dr. Darshani remains committed to patient-centered, holistic healing and continues to serve with unwavering dedication. Her leadership and clinical contributions make Erundeniya Ayurveda Hospital a trusted name in Sri Lanka’s traditional medical landscape.
